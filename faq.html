FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), and other popular payment gateways. All payments are securely processed.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only offer nationwide shipping within Pakistan. We are working to expand our shipping options to international customers in the future. Please check back for updates!

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy on all eligible items. Products must be in their original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached. For more details, please visit our Refund Policy page.

How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can use this number to track the status of your delivery.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by filling out the form on our Contact Us page or by emailing us at support@rakh.pk. Our team will get back to you as soon as possible.
